THE PROBLEM

In Canada, we waste over 2.2 million tonnes of edible food anually. A central part of that problem is that consumers often confuse food labels or simply forget the expiry date of certain items, leading to unneccessary waste. This food waste ends up costing Canadians an incredible 17 billion dollars every year.




THE SOLUTION

Memofood aims to solve this problem by partnering with supermarkets to provide users with their products' expiration dates, which will be automatically synced to their Memofood account. This way, when items approach their expiration dates, users will be reminded by the Memofood app by notifications to consume their product.
